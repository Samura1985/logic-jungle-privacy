Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

Logic Jungle does not require user accounts and does not ask users to provide personal information such as a name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, or precise location.

The game may include third-party advertising and mediation services, such as Unity LevelPlay, ironSource, and Unity Ads. These services may collect or receive certain data from the device in order to show ads, measure ad performance, prevent fraud, and keep advertising services working properly.

The data processed by these third-party services may include device or other identifiers, such as the Android Advertising ID, IP address, device information, app interactions, ad interactions, and diagnostic information. Approximate location may also be inferred from the IP address.

Logic Jungle does not sell personal information. Data handled by third-party advertising services is processed according to their own privacy policies and settings. Users can reset or delete their Android Advertising ID in their device settings.

If future versions of the game change how data is collected, shared, or used, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly.
